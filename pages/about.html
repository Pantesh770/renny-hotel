About Renny

Smart hotel recommendations powered by intelligent matching.

Our Story

Making Hotel Discovery Effortless

Renny was created as a final year project with a simple mission: help travelers find the perfect hotel without the overwhelming noise of traditional booking platforms. Instead of showing you every option, we use a transparent weighted scoring system to recommend hotels that truly match your preferences.

Our algorithm considers your desired location, budget, preferred amenities, star rating expectations, and community reviews to generate a personalized Match Score for each hotel. The result? Quick, relevant recommendations you can trust.

How It Works

The Recommendation Engine

Our scoring system assigns weights to four key factors: Location (40%), Budget (30%), Amenities (20%), and Rating (10%). Each hotel is scored against your preferences, sorted by match percentage, and presented to you with full transparency.

40% Location — Match to your chosen destination
30% Budget — Closeness to your price range
20% Amenities — Availability of your preferred features
10% Rating — Star rating matching your expectations
